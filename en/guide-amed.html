“Black volcanic sand, Agung on the horizon, dived from the shore - and you are among the corals.Bali without Instagram filters."

Amed is a fishing village in northeast Bali.The capital of diving and freediving on the island.Black beaches, calm water, coral reefs right from the shore, views of Agung.Divers, freedivers and those who are tired of the southern chaos live here.Minimal infrastructure, maximum beauty.Average age 25-40.

Laundries at guesthouses

There is no nightlife in Amed.In the evening - dinner in the warung, beer (Bintang 25-35K) on the shore, stars.Clubs, bars with DJs - zero.This is a village.For parties:Seminyak(3 hours) orChangu(3 hours).
