“Rocks over the ocean, world surfing, sunset from the monkey temple, cliff clubs over turquoise water.Bali is for the free."

Uluwatu is the western part of the Bukit Peninsula.World surfing (Padang Padang, Suluban, Bingin), cliffs overlooking the Indian Ocean, cliff clubs and bars.It's quieter and more beautiful here than on the west coast.For surfers, photographers and those who need the ocean and freedom.Growing rapidly: new villas and cliff clubs every year.

Looking for tranquil beaches, resorts and seafood?Lookguide to Bukit and Nusa Dua.

🌋 Volcanoes and mountains nearby▸

Uluwatu is in the south, far from the volcanoes.Nearest:Gunung Batur(Kintamani, 2.5-3 hours).Gunung Agung— 3.5 hours.

🌾 Rice terraces nearby▸

There are no rice terraces in Uluwatu - it is a rocky peninsula.Nearest:Jatiluwih(2 hours),Tegallalang(2 hours, via Ubud).

💡 Bukit Lifehacks

🏖 Melasti - come in the morning.Crowds from buses during the day
🌅 Kecak dance - buy tickets in advance, sit on the left side (best view)
🐒 Uluwatu - hide glasses and phones.Monkeys STEAL!
