“White sand, turquoise water, rocks over the ocean, sunset from the monkey temple. Bali as you imagined it.”

Uluwatu is the Bukit Peninsula in southern Bali. The best beaches of the island, world surfing, cliffs overlooking the Indian Ocean. It's quieter and more beautiful here than on the west coast. For those who need the ocean, not a party. Growing rapidly: new villas and cliff clubs every year.

🌋 Volcanoes and mountains nearby

Uluwatu is in the south, far from the volcanoes. Nearest: Gunung Batur (Kintamani, 2.5-3 hours). Gunung Agung — 3.5 hours.

🌾 Rice terraces nearby

There are no rice terraces in Uluwatu - it is a rocky peninsula. Nearest: Jatiluwih (2 hours), Tegallalang (2 hours, via Ubud).

🤫 Secret of the area
Nyang Nyang Beach is one of the most beautiful and empty beaches in Bali. 500 steps down, white sand for a kilometer, not a single person. The sunset here is like on a desert island.
⚠️ Cons
You can't go anywhere without a scooter. Grab/Gojek don't work well. Few shops and restaurants. There are hundreds of steps down to the beaches. Far from the airport due to traffic jams. The infrastructure is developing, but is still poor. The roads are narrow and of poor quality.
