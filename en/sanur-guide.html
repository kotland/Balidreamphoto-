“Bicycle along the ocean, breakfast with a view of Nusa Penida, swimming without waves. Sanur is Bali for those who have had their fill of chaos.”

Sanur - east coast, calm water, long promenade, family atmosphere. Retired expats, families with children and divers live here. The reef protects you from the waves - you can swim! Close to the airport, ferry to Nusa Penida. Average age 35-55.

💻 Coworking spaces

There are few dedicated coworking spaces in Sanur. They work from a cafe (Genius, Dusk Blue - good WiFi). For a serious coworking space: Canggu (1 hour) or Denpasar (20 min).

🏄 Surfing

Sanur Reef
Reef spot for intermediate level. Right break, need a boat to the spot.

Another reef spot. Average level. The waves are smaller than in Canggu.

Surfing in Sanur is not the main thing. The waves are weak and reefy. For a good surf: Canggu (1 hour), Keramas (30 min).

🛵 Transport

• Bike: best transportation in Sanur! The embankment is flat. Rent from 50K/day
• Scooter: 600K-800K/month
• Grab/Gojek: from 10K, works well
• To the airport: 25-35 min, 80-150K
• Ferry to Nusa Penida: pier right in Sanur, 200K, 30 min

🤫 Secret of the area
Pasar Sindhu Night Market is the best street food night market in southern Bali. Satay for 15K, martabak for 20K, fresh juice for 10K. The atmosphere of real Indonesia - without tourist extra charges.
⚠️ Cons
Boring for young people. Little nightlife. The beach is unclear at high tide. “Babushkin District” - the average age of expats is 50+. WiFi is weaker than in Canggu. Few coworking spaces.
