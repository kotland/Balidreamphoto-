“Brunch in a designer cafe, shopping in boutiques, sunset in a beach club with a cocktail. Bali is for those who love a beautiful life."

Seminyak is Bali's most glamorous area. The best restaurants, boutiques, beach clubs and nightlife. It's home to expats on a budget, models, food bloggers and entrepreneurs. Closer to the airport than Canggu. Average age 28-40.

🤿 Diving

There are no dive sites in Seminyak. Nearest: Nusa Penida (2 hours, manti), Tulamben (3 hours, USS Liberty). Dive shops organize day trips with pickup.

🫁 Freediving

Not in Seminyak - the closest one is: Amed (3 hours), Nusa Penida. Schools from Canggu organize trips.

🐠 Snorkeling

Not in Seminyak - muddy water, waves. Nearest: Sanur (30 min), Nusa Penida (day trip).

🏖 Beaches without waves

All Seminyak beaches have waves. For a relaxing swim: Sanur (30 min) or Nusa Dua (45 min).

🤫 Secret of the area
Jl. Drupadi and Jl. Nakula - quiet streets in the center of Seminyak with local warungs and small villas at prices 2 times lower than the main streets. All luxury hotels are within a 5-minute walk.
⚠️ Cons
Expensive. Traffic jams are constant (especially Jl. Raya Seminyak). There are no sidewalks - it is dangerous to walk. Beaches for surfing, not for swimming. Noisy. Parking is a problem. Prices in restaurants are the same as in Europe.
