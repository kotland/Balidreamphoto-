“Surf in the morning, work from a cafe overlooking the rice fields in the afternoon, sunset with friends in the evening. Repeat."

Canggu is the unofficial capital of digital nomads. If you work online, create content, surf, or just want to live in a balance between productivity and fun, this is the place. Many Russians, Australians, Europeans. Average age 25-35.

🤿 Diving

There is no diving in Canggu itself - muddy water, strong currents. Nearest dive sites:

Nusa Penida (1.5 hours)
Manta Point, Crystal Bay. Manti, mola-mola (July-October). Day trip from 2M.
Tulamben (2.5 hours)
USS Liberty is a sunken ship. Dive trip from 1.5M with transfer.

Schools in Canggu: Blue Season Bali, Bali Diving Academy - organize trips with pickup.

🐠 Snorkeling

In Canggu there are no waves and muddy water. Nearest: Nusa Penida (1.5 hours, day trip from 500K), Amed (2.5 hours), Sanur (reefs offshore, 45 min).

🏖 Beaches without waves

There are no calm beaches for swimming in Canggu - there are waves and strong currents everywhere. Nearest: Sanur (45 min) - calm water, protected by a coral reef. Nusa Dua (1 hour) - turquoise lagoon.

🤫 Secret of the area
Pererenan is “Canggu in 2 years.” Quieter, cheaper, more beautiful. Those who know are already moving there. The best villas and cafes open here.
⚠️ Cons
Traffic jams during rush hour (16-19). The ocean is dangerous for swimming (strong currents). Prices are rising every year. Lots of construction and noise. During the rainy season (November-March) the roads are flooded. Sometimes there is trash on the beaches.
