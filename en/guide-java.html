🔥 Blue flame
“The blue flame of Ijen at 2 am.Dawn on Bromo among the clouds.Borobudur at dawn.Java is another planet."

The neighboring island has volcanoes with blue flames, giant waterfalls, ancient temples.A 3-5 day excursion that will change your understanding of Indonesia.East Java - volcanoes and waterfalls.Central Java - temples and culture.

🌋 Volcano Bromo▸

Mount Bromo Sunrise (Penanjakan viewpoint)
Dawn over a sea of ​​sand, the Bromo crater smokes below, Martian landscapes all around.Departure at 2-3 am by jeep, dawn at 5:30.One of the most spectacular sunrises in the world.
📍 Maps· Tour from $40-80/person
Bromo Crater
After dawn - descent into the “sea of ​​sand” in a jeep, climb the stairs (253 steps) to the smoking crater.Sulfur smell, incredible views.
📍 Maps· Entry 320K (foreigner)
Savanna Teletubbies Hill
The green hills near Bromo are like something out of a cartoon.Photo shoot is a must!
📍 Maps
⚠️ It's cold!
On Bromo at night 5-10°C!Take a warm jacket, hat, gloves.In Bali, don’t forget to buy before your trip - then you’ll have nowhere to go.

🔥 Volcano Ijen▸

Kawah Ijen Blue Fire
The blue flame is burning sulfur gas, visible only in the dark.The ascent begins at 1-2 am (3 km up).Gas mask is required!Below is the largest acid lake in the world, turquoise in color.
📍 Maps· Entry 100K, mask: 50K rental
Sulfur miners
Local miners carry baskets of sulfur (70-80 kg!) along a steep path.You can buy sulfur souvenirs on the way back.
💡 Combo Ijen + Bromo
Classic route: Bali → Ijen (night) → transfer → Bromo (night) → Surabaya.3 days / 2 nights.Tour from $120-200/person.Or vice versa, through Surabaya.
⚠️ Security
Sulfur gases are really dangerous!Gas mask is required (available for rent).Don't go down to the lake if the wind is in your direction.A flashlight is a must, the path is steep in the dark.

💧 Tumpak Sevu Waterfall▸

Tumpak Sewu Waterfall
“A Thousand Cascades” is a giant semicircular waterfall 120m high.Water falls from everywhere, like an amphitheater.One of the most impressive waterfalls in the world!
📍 Maps· Login 20K
Descent to the foot
The path down is steep and slippery (30-45 min).Below is a cave behind a waterfall, a rainbow in the spray.You will get completely wet!
Coban Sewu (nearby)
Another waterfall is 10 minutes away.Thinner, but also beautiful.Can be combined.
📍 Maps
💡 Advice
• Come early in the morning (7-8) - less people, better light.
• A waterproof phone case is a must!
• Shoes with tread - the trail is very slippery.
• Nearby is the Semeru volcano in the background, visible from the top observation deck.

🏛 Borobudur▸

Borobudur Temple
The largest Buddhist temple in the world (9th century).2672 bas-reliefs, 504 Buddha statues.The sunrise against the backdrop of volcanoes is unforgettable.UNESCO World Heritage.
📍 Maps· 350K (foreigner) / Sunrise tour from 475K
💡 Sunrise tour
Entrance at dawn (4:30) through the Manohara Hotel inside the complex.More expensive, but the temple without crowds + sunrise from the top tier.Book a day in advance!

🏛 Prambanan▸

Prambanan Temple
The largest Hindu temple in Indonesia (9th century).The three main towers are dedicated to Shiva, Vishnu and Brahma.The height of the central temple is 47m.The sunset here is magical.
📍 Maps· 350K (foreigner)
💡 Combo with Borobudur
Both temples are in the Yogyakarta area.Sunrise in Borobudur, sunset in Prambanan - a perfect day.Combo ticket is cheaper.Jogja is an interesting city in itself: street art, batik, street food.

🏙 Yogyakarta - city▸

Malioboro Street
The main pedestrian street of Yogyakarta - street food, souvenirs, batik, street musicians.In the evening there is a special atmosphere.
📍 Maps· For free
Kraton (Sultan's Palace)
The current palace of the Sultan of Yogyakarta (XVIII century).Traditional Javanese architecture, gamelan orchestra, museum.
📍 Maps · 15K
Taman Sari (Water Castle)
Royal garden-pool of the 18th century.Underground tunnels, photogenic ruins, artificial lakes.
📍 Maps · 15K
Sewu Temple
Buddhist temple complex near Prambanan (249 small temples).Almost no tourists.
📍 Maps· Included in Prambanan ticket

🌀 Activities and adventures▸

Jomblang Cave
Rope descent 60m down into the cave.An underground river, a canyon and a “ray of light” through a hole in the ceiling - an incredible sight!
📍 Maps· 500K, book in advance
Mount Merapi Jeep Tour
Jeep tour through the lava fields of the most active volcano in Indonesia.Ruins of villages covered with ash in 2010.
📍 Maps · 350-450K
Dieng Plateau
High mountain plateau (2000m) - colored lakes, ancient Hindu temples (8th century), geysers.Take your jacket!
📍 Maps · 20K
Punthuk Setumbu Sunrise
Hill near Borobudur - sunrise with a view of the temple, Merapi and Merbabu volcanoes.Iconic shot!
📍 Maps · 30K

💡 Lifehacks▸

🗺 Optimal route for 4-5 days
Day 1:Bali → ferry to Banyuwangi → Ijen night → blue flame
Day 2:Transfer to Bromo (5-6 hours), overnight at Bromo
Day 3:Dawn on Bromo → Tumpak Sewu → night in Malang
Day 4:Malang → Yogyakarta (train) → Borobudur
Day 5:Prambanan → plane to Bali
⚡ Important to know
• Java is a Muslim island.Dress more modestly, especially near temples.
• Food is VERY cheap: nasi goreng from 8-10K!
• Trains in Java are excellent and cheap (Traveloka for booking).
• Grab/GoJek operate in cities.
• English is understood worse than in Bali.
