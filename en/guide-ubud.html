"Woke up in the jungle, yoga at dawn, coffee overlooking rice terraces, gamelan sounds from the temple in the evening."

Ubud is the cultural heart of Bali.Jungles, temples, rice fields, yoga retreats and the best vegetarian food on the island.This is where artists, spiritual seekers and remote workers live who don't need the ocean.Average age 30-45.Quieter and cheaper than Canggu.

🏄 Surfing▸

There is no surfing in Ubud - it is a mountainous area with no access to the ocean.Nearest:Changu(1-1.5 hours),Keramas(45 min, advanced level).

🤿Underwater▸

There is no coast in Ubud.Nearest:Padangbai(1.5 hours) - Blue Lagoon, reefs.With clothes(2 hours) — USS Liberty.Nusa Penida(2+ hours) - manti.

Not in Ubud - the closest one is:Amed(2 hours),Nusa Penida(2.5 hours).Schools organize transfers.

🏖 Beaches without waves▸

There are no beaches in Ubud - it is a mountainous area.Nearest:Sanur(1 hour) - calm water, ideal for swimming.

🤫 Secret of the area
Penestanan is a quiet area on a hill above Ubud.Villas among rice fields, not a single tourist.Prices are 2 times lower than the center.The best spot for long-stay.
⚠️ Cons
Humidity 85-95% - things become moldy.There are a lot of monkeys (they steal glasses and phones).No beach.Far from the airport.Little nightlife.Mosquitoes during the rainy season.Narrow roads with monkeys.

💡 Ubud Lifehacks

🌅 Campuhan Ridge Walk - arrive by 5:30.By 9:00 there are already crowds
🐒 Monkey Forest - remove glasses, earrings, water bottles.Monkeys steal!
💍 Celuk Silver - compare prices in several workshops.Proof 925 = real
🌿 Tegallalang - early in the morning (before 9:00).Buses arrive at 11:00
