“Rocks fall into the turquoise ocean, a manta ray swims beneath me, T-Rex Beach is like from another planet.Nusa Penida is Bali on steroids.”

Nusa Penida is an island 30 minutes by speedboat from Sanur.The most dramatic landscapes of Bali: cliffs, turquoise water, manta rays, Kelingking Beach.The island is developing quickly, but is still wild: bad roads, minimal infrastructure, but incredible beauty.For day trips or adventurous long-stays.

🛵 Transport▸

• Scooter:70-100K/day (roads are BAD - potholes, dirt roads, hills. Experience required!)
• Grab/Gojek:doesn't work
• Speedboat from Bali:from Sanur, 200-300K, 30 min.Several flights per day.
• Day tour with driver:500-700K/day (recommended - roads are dangerous)
• Car rental with driver:600-800K/day

⚠️ The roads in Nusa Penida are the most dangerous in Bali.Steep descents, lack of fences, holes.If you have no experience on a scooter -take a car with driver.

🌊 Secret places▸

🤫 Secret of the area
Peguyangan Waterfall is not a waterfall, but a 700-step staircase to a sacred spring in the rock.Along the way there are blue pools with salt water at different levels of the rock.Few people come - but this is one of the most magical places in all of Bali.Come early in the morning.
⚠️ Cons
Very bad roads (one of the worst in Indonesia).Dangerous descents to the beaches (hundreds of steps, no guardrails).Weak WiFi.Few shops and restaurants.No medicine (evacuation to Bali).Strong currents in the sea mean swimming is not possible everywhere.During the rainy season, roads wash out.There are a lot of tourists at Kelingking 10:00-15:00.

💡 Lifehacks▸

💡 Lifehacks of Nusa Penida

⛴ Boat from Sanur ~30-45 min.Tickets from 150K one way
🛵 The roads are HORRIBLE!Steep climbs, gravel.Only for experienced bike riders
📱 Internet is weak.Download offline maps in advance
🕐 Minimum 2 days!You can’t do it in 1 day - the island is big, the roads are long
🌊 Angel's Billabong - ONLY in low tide.Check the tides!
💎 Diamond Beach - arrive at 7-8 am.By 10:00 - crowds and heat
