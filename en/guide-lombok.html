“Rinjani - 3726 meters.Dawn over the crater lake.Wild beaches without a single person.This is not Bali - this is next level.”

Bali's wild neighbor is the Rinjani volcano, pink beaches, world-class surf spots.Fewer tourists, more adventure.Lombok is for those for whom Bali has become “too much”.And Sumbawa is even further: whale sharks in the Gulf of Saleh.

🌋 Rinjani Volcano▸

Mount Rinjani Trek (3726м)
The second highest volcano in Indonesia.The sunrise over the Segara Anak crater lake is one of the most beautiful views in Southeast Asia.Trekking 2-3 days with tents.
📍 Maps· Tour from $180-300/person
Senaru Crater Rim (2D1N)
A shortened version - climbing to the edge of the crater in 2 days.Dawn at 2639m, view of the lake.Physically it’s easier.
📍 Maps· Tour from $150/person
Sembalun Hot Springs
Hot springs at the foot of the volcano.After trekking - ideal for muscles.
📍 Maps · 20K
⚠️ Important
Trekking ONLY with a licensed guide (mandatory by law).Book through trusted agencies: Rinjani Trekking Planner, Rudy Trekker, Adi Trekker.Season: April-November.The trails are closed during the rainy season.

🐋Whale sharks (Sumbawa)▸

Saleh Bay, Sumbawa
Whale sharks are the largest fish in the world, up to 12m!Swimming near fishing platforms (bagan) in the Gulf of Saleh.Snorkeling in calm waters.Season: March-November.
📍 Maps· Tour from $80-150
💡 How to get there
From Lombok: ferry to Sumbawa (1.5 hours, Kayangan → Poto Tano), then 3-4 hours by car to Saleh Bay.Or a 2-3 day tour package from Lombok through an agency.Better for 2 nights - morning trips to the sharks.

💡 Lifehacks▸

💡 Lombok vs Bali
• Lombok is a Muslim island (as opposed to Hindu Bali).More conservative.
• Alcohol is more expensive and less available in local warungs.
• The roads are worse, but new ones are being built.
• Prices are 30-40% lower than Bali.
• Internet is weaker, especially in the south.
🏍 Transport
•Bike: 60-80K/day in Kuta Lombok.
• Grab only operates in Mataram and the airport.In Kuta there are local drivers.
• To Pink Beach - only by bike or with a driver (dirt road).
