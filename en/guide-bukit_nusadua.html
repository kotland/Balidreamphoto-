“White sand without waves, grilled fish at sunset in Jimbaran, world-class resorts.Bali is for those who want peace and beauty.”

The southern part of the Bukit Peninsula - from Melasti and Balangan to Nusa Dua and Jimbaran.There are calm beaches with turquoise water, 5-star resorts, family fun and the best seafood on the island.Ideal for families, couples and those who want a luxury holiday without the fuss.

🏖 Beaches▸

Melasti Beach
The most beautiful beach in Bukit.Turquoise water between white rocks, gentle entrance.It's calm in the morning - arrive before 10.
📍 Ungasan · Parking 5-10K, sunbed 100-150K
Pandawa Beach
A calm beach with a gentle entrance - ideal for children.There are almost no waves.Kayaks, sun loungers.Along the way there are statues of Pandavas in the rocks.
📍 Kutuh · Entrance 20K, deck chair 50K
Geger Beach (Nusa Dua)
Best beach for swimming!Turquoise lagoon, white sand, almost no waves.Public entrance next to the resorts.
📍 Maps· For free
Balangan Beach
Golden sand, rocks on the sides.Quiet and beautiful.Warungi is right on the beach.
📍 Maps· Login 10K
Jimbaran Beach
Wide beach with soft sand.Famous for seafood dinners at sunset - tables right on the sand.
📍 Jimbaran Bay
Nusa Dua Beach
Well-maintained resort beach.Calm water, water sports, path along the shore.
📍 ITDC Complex

🍤 Restaurants and seafood▸

Jimbaran - Seafood on the Beach

The main feature of Jimbaran is the fish restaurants right on the sand.You choose fish/lobster from a bucket, grill it, eat at sunset with your feet in the sand.

💡 Lifehacks▸

🏖 Melasti - come in the morning.Crowds from buses during the day
🏖 Pandawa - gentle entrance, ideal for children.Kayaks for rent
🍤 Jimbaran seafood - book a table by the water at sunset.Dinner from 150K
☀️ Bukit is drier than Ubud/Canggu during the rainy season - the best area in winter
🏨 Nusa Dua - resorts often provide access to the beach for a minimum order at the bar
