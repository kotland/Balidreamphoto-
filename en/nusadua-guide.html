“Turquoise lagoon, white sand, 5-star resort, cocktail by the pool. Bali without chaos - for those who know what they want.”

Nusa Dua is a resort enclave in southeast Bali. The best beaches for swimming, 5-star resorts, clean sidewalks, security. Families with children, golfers and those who want a “normal” beach holiday live here. A little Balinese flavor, a lot of comfort.

💻 Coworking spaces

There are no coworking spaces in Nusa Dua - this is a resort area. They operate from a cafe or resort lobby. For serious work: Sanur (20 min) or Canggu (1 hour).

The choice of vegetarian establishments is limited. For variety: Sanur (20 min) or Seminyak (45 min).

🌱 Vegan places

There are no designated vegan restaurants in Nusa Dua. Resorts usually offer vegan options upon request. For plant-based cafe: Sanur (20 min), Seminyak (45 min).

🏄 Surfing

Sri Lanka (reef spot)
Reef break for intermediate+ level. Right wave, need a boat.
📍 Offshore Nusa Dua · Boat 150K

The beaches of Nusa Dua are protected by a reef - there are almost no waves. For surfing: Uluwatu (30 min), Canggu (1 hour).

🤫 Secret of the area
Bualu Village is a village right at the entrance to BTDC. Locals live here, prices are 3-5 times lower than resort prices. Warungs for 25K, fruit at the market, laundry for 7K/kg. 5 minutes walk - and you are again on a 5-star beach.
⚠️ Cons
Sterile - no Balinese flavor. Expensive (resort prices). No nightlife. Bored after 3 days. Far from cultural attractions. There are few warungs and shops inside BTDC.
