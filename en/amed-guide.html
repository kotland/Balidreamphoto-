“Black volcanic sand, Agung on the horizon, dived from the shore - and you are among the corals. Bali without Instagram filters."

Amed is a fishing village in northeast Bali. The capital of diving and freediving on the island. Black beaches, calm water, coral reefs right from the shore, views of Agung. Divers, freedivers and those who are tired of the southern chaos live here. Minimal infrastructure, maximum beauty. Average age 25-40.

💻 Coworking spaces

There are no coworking spaces in Amed. WiFi in cafes and guesthouses - 5-20 Mbps (unstable). For serious work: Canggu (3 hours) or Ubud (2 hours). Many remote workers use Starlink or a 4G router.

🏄 Surfing

There is no surfing in Amed - calm water, sheltered bays. Nearest: Keramas (1.5 hours) - powerful waves, advanced level. Canggu (3 hours) - for all levels.

🛍 Shopping

There is practically no shopping in Amed. Small souvenirs, beachwear. For shopping: Ubud (2 hours) or Seminyak (3 hours).

🛒 Grocery stores

Minimarket (Alfamart / Indomaret)
Small shops with basic supplies: water, snacks, noodles, hygiene products.
📍 On the way Amed

There are no imported products. For European goods: order online or travel to Denpasar.

👕 Laundries

Laundries at guesthouses
Most hotels and guesthouses do laundry for 5-8K/kg. There is no self-service.

🎉 Clubs and nightlife

There is no nightlife in Amed. In the evening - dinner in the warung, beer (Bintang 25-35K) on the shore, stars. Clubs, bars with DJs - zero. This is a village. For parties: Seminyak (3 hours) or Canggu (3 hours).

🏥 Clinics and pharmacies

Amed Clinic
Small clinic for basic problems. English speaking doctor.
📍 Amed · Reception from 150K
RSUD Karangasem Hospital
Nearest hospital. Basic, but there is ER.
📍 Karangasem · 30 min from Amed

For serious medicine: BIMC or Kasih Ibu in Denpasar (2.5-3 hours). Have insurance with evacuation!

The mother of all temples in Bali. A huge complex on the slope of Agung.
📍 1 hour from Amed · Entrance 60K
Tirta Gangga
Water Palace of the Rajah. Fountains, pools, koi carp. Beautiful!
📍 30 min from Amed · Entrance 40K

🇷🇺 Russian community

There are few Russians in Amed, but the freediving community is international - there are Russian instructors. Search in chats: “Bali East”, “Freediving Bali”. There are no Russian restaurants.

🎒 Kindergartens and schools

There are no kindergartens or schools for expats in Amed. The area is not for families with school-age children. Nearest schools: Ubud (2 hours) - Green School, Pelangi.

🤫 Secret of the area
During the full moon, the nocturnal inhabitants of the reef come out to Jemeluk Bay. Night snorkeling with a flashlight - octopuses, mantis shrimp, sleeping parrotfish. Ask the dive center about night snorkeling. Unforgettable.
⚠️ Cons
Very far from the airport (3 hours). Poor WiFi. There are few shops, no imported products. No nightlife. No gyms. Narrow road along the coast (dangerous at night). Few medical services. During the rainy season, the road sometimes gets washed away.
