“Fog over the lakes, strawberries straight from the garden, an 80-meter waterfall, hot springs and a pine forest - all in one day.Bali as few people know it.”

Mountains, lakes, waterfalls, strawberry farms, temperature 17-22°C.Includes the areas of Kintamani, Bedugul, Munduk - between Ubud and Lovina.A completely different Bali: without beaches, without heat, but with mountain air, volcanoes and the atmosphere of a mountain village.You can come for the day or stay overnight in glamping.

🛵 Transport▸

🚗 A car with a driver is the best option!$60-80/day for a full day.Serpentine, 2-3 hours from the south of Bali.The driver knows the road and will wait at every point.Book through your hotel or Klook.
🛵 Bike:For experienced people, it’s possible, but the serpentines are steep and in some places there are no bump stops.It's dangerous in rain and fog.Refills are rare.Not recommended for beginners!
🚫 Grab/GoJek DO NOT work!There are no online taxis in the mountains.The only option is to negotiate with local drivers or order a transfer in advance.
🚐 Excursions:The easiest way is a day tour from Ubud or Canggu via Klook/GetYourGuide (from 500K IDR).Includes transport, guide, lunch.
⛽ Gas stations:Rare!Fuel up in the south before going up.In the mountains, gasoline is sold in bottles near the road (more expensive).
⏱ Travel time:Denpasar/Airport → Kintamani: 2-2.5 hours.Ubud → Kintamani: 1-1.5 hours.Canggu → Bedugul: 1.5-2 hours.Kintamani → Munduk: 1.5 hours.

👕 Laundries▸

Laundries at guesthouses
There are no separate laundries in the mountains.Laundry - through your guesthouse or homestay.Almost everyone offers a service: you drop it off in the morning, pick it up in the evening or the next day.
📍 At your home · from 7-15K/kg
Glampings and eco-resorts
Black Lava, Toteme, Munduk Moding all have a laundry service for guests.Prices are slightly higher (from 20K/kg).

💡 Drying in the mountains is slow - high humidity!Plan your washing in advance, it may take 1.5-2 days to dry.

💡 Lifehacks▸

🥶 17-22°C at the top - take a jacket and long pants!It can be 15°C at night
🌅 Visit waterfalls in the morning (8-10am) - fewer people, better light
🛵 The roads in the mountains are serpentine - be careful on a bike, especially in the rain
💰 Near tourist waterfalls - inflated prices for guides.Official entrance is cheaper
🌧 Rainy season (November-March): the waterfalls are more powerful, but the roads are slippery and foggy
📸 Handara Gate: arrive at 7 am - after 10 the queue for a photo is 30+ minutes
🍓 La Fresa - Japanese strawberry varieties.You can eat straight from the garden!
🎨 Paintings on volcanic sand - a unique souvenir!Buddha motifs, temples.Sellers along the road in Kintamani district
🧺 Picnic at Handara - picnic set 150-170K IDR!On the lawn overlooking the mountains and fog.Also picnic at Rumah Gemuk near Lake Beratan
🎾 Tennis court at Handara Golf & Resort - you can rent a court even if you are not a hotel guest
🚗 Renting a car with a driver is the best way to get there!Serpentine, 2-3 hours from the south.Car with driver $60-80/day, includes waiting
☕ Coffee shopping around the caldera: all cafes are next to each other on Penelokan Road.You can get around 3-4 in a morning!
🏊 After trekking to Batur - hot springs!The best way to recover

🤫 Secret of the area
There are hot springs in Munduk that the guidebooks don't mention - just ask the local "air panas".Also: after 15:00 there is almost no one at Handara Gate, and the morning fog creates a Middle-earth atmosphere.Grilled Corn Curbside is 10K and the best food in the mountains.
⚠️ Cons
No beaches.No nightlife.Far from the airport (2-3 hours).Serpentine roads are not for beginners on a bike.Few restaurants and shops.It's cold, especially at night.Weak WiFi.During the rainy season, roads can be dangerous.Little medical infrastructure.
