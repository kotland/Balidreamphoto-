“Brunch in a designer cafe, shopping in boutiques, sunset in a beach club with a cocktail.Bali is for those who love a beautiful life.”

Seminyak is Bali's most glamorous area.The best restaurants, boutiques, beach clubs and nightlife.It's home to expats on a budget, models, food bloggers and entrepreneurs.Closer to the airport than Canggu.Average age 28-40.

🛒 Grocery stores▸

Pepito Market
A piece of Europe in Bali.Parmesan, jamon, wine, fresh meat for cooking.Fruits are sometimes perfect.Imported household chemicals, cosmetics.Indomaret is more expensive, but the range is incomparably better.
📍 Maps
Coco Supermarket
Large supermarket with good imports.Pepito is cheaper, but the range is almost the same.Meat, seafood, cheeses, wine, household chemicals.
📍 Maps
Frestive Supermarket
Budget version of Pepito.Everything for cooking: meat, seafood, fruits, vegetables.Household chemicals, dishes.Less imports, but better prices.Ideal for daily shopping.
📍 Maps
Indomaret / Alfamart
Same thing, different signs.Snacks, drinks, basic amenities (wash, shave).On every corner, 24/7.There is no meat or import - that's what Pepito is for.
📍 Available in every area
Circle K
24-hour mini market.Snacks, cold drinks, coffee to go.Saves at night and early in the morning.
📍 Available in every area
